Welcome to Tabletop Smash

Tools to make your tabletop games easier

Free... for now and forever

There are a lot of good tools out there but there are always catches. Somewhere along the line there is a monthly subscription. Tabletop Smash isn't doing that, all the tools are free and available to you. Always.

Easily create characters

One of the real pains of tabletop games is creating the characters. Not because it is hard but because there are so many little details you have to keep track of. Does a Cleric get a bonus to this, and when does the bard get to have an extra turn? (never... the bard never has a extra turn).

So Tabletop Smash does away with all that. It knows the rules so you can focus on just making a kick-ass character.

Share your creations

Tabletop games are community driven so why not give something back yourself. You have made a kick-ass Elf make it public so everyone can see it. Someone else can inspiration from your ideas, and all it takes is the click of a button.
